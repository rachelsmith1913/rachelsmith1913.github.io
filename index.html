Rachel Smith is a producer, host, filmmaker, screenwriter, and sports fanatic. At 22 years old Rachel is also the founder of a television show called The Culture which is filmed and broadcasted from the University of Miami. Her work experiences include Bleacher Report, the Miami Super Bowl Host Committee, and the Miami Dolphins to name a few. As a soon to be graduate from the University of Miami, Rachel plans to search for employment opportunities that allow her to tell stories that bring about positive change in the world. According to her, this will be done by telling stories that obliterate negative stereotypes, inspire the youth and cause a ripple of positive action. She invites you to follow her journey and is excited to see exactly what the future holds. ☺️
